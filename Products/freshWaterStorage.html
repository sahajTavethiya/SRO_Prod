    Fresh Water Storage

    • large amounts stored naturally in ice, embedded in soil and vegetation, underground in aquifers, or on the surface in lakes and wetlands
    • Keep containers in a place with a cool temperature (50°F –70°F).
    • Water can be stored in the atmosphere, on the surface of the Earth, or underground
    • These water storage areas are most commonly known as reservoirs
    • Storage may also be a combination of natural and built (sometimes also called green and gray solutions).
